Our Story

About the Temple

✦✦✦
Est. 2005

The Shiv Durga Hindu Temple is a vibrant center of Hindu worship, culture, and community — a sacred home for devotees seeking spiritual solace and divine connection.

Founded with the blessing of Lord Shiva and Goddess Durga, our temple serves as a beacon of Hindu traditions, rituals, and festivals. We welcome all devotees — from all walks of life — to experience the peace and grace that our sacred space offers.

Our temple conducts daily pujas, abhishekams, and special ceremonies throughout the year, guided by our learned priests who follow Vedic traditions with devotion and authenticity.

Our Presiding Deities

✦✦✦
☽

Lord Shiva

Mahadev – The Supreme Being

The cosmic destroyer and transformer, Lord Shiva represents the ultimate reality. He is the lord of ascetics, the destroyer of evil, and the eternal source of benevolence and grace.

मंत्र: ॐ नमः शिवाय
ॐ

Shiv & Durga

United in Divine Power

The sacred union of Shiva and Shakti — the masculine and feminine cosmic energies — represents the perfect balance of creation. Together they bless all devotees with protection, wisdom, and liberation.

मंत्र: ॐ शिव दुर्गायै नमः
⚡

Goddess Durga

Shakti – The Divine Mother

The warrior goddess, Durga embodies the divine feminine power. She is the protector of the righteous, destroyer of evil forces, and the nurturing mother who guides her devotees toward liberation.

मंत्र: ॐ दुं दुर्गायै नमः

🦚 Lord Kartikeya

Temple Leadership

✦✦✦

Guided by unwavering devotion and decades of Vedic knowledge, our founders lead SDHT with heart and spirit.

Head Priest & Co-Founder

Mahant Shashtri Ji

Mahant HH Shri Shantiprasadacharya

Mahant Shashtri Ji is a highly qualified Vedic Scholar with a Master's degree in Sanskrit languages and deep expertise in chanting all 4 Vedas. He has decades of training in performing authentic Vedic rituals and ceremonies.

As the guiding spiritual force of SDHT, he travels across the United States and internationally — including Canada, England, Europe, and Mexico — to bless devotees and perform sacred pujas.

Smt. Uma Devi Ji

Smt. Uma Devi Nautiyal

As the Founder and President of Shiv Durga Hindu Temple, Uma Ji is the driving force behind this sacred journey. She holds everything and everyone together with her tireless dedication and grace.
